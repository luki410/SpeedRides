Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About the Car Rental Booking Process on Our Website: Answers to Common Concerns and Inquiries.

How old do I have to be to rent a car from your website?

To rent a car from our website, you must be at least 21 years old and have a valid driver's license. However, some high-performance vehicles may have additional age requirements.

How long can I rent a car for?

We offer flexible rental options to fit your schedule and needs. Our rentals can range from a single day to several weeks, depending on your preferences. Contact us for more information on our rental options.

What happens if I damage the car during the rental period?

We understand that accidents happen, and we have a comprehensive insurance policy to protect both you and our vehicles. If you damage the car during the rental period, please contact us immediately to report the incident and we will provide you with guidance on the next steps to take. Additional charges may apply depending on the extent of the damage.
